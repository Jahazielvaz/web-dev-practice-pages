Tooltips

A message that appears when you point your cursor at something is called a tooltip. Be it a paragraph, header, image or any element, the title attribute is used to provide additional information about it. It is very useful to elaborate abbreviations or add some context. For images, you must use an alt attribute as there is no guarantee that the title attribute is presented to assistive technology users. The title can be of any text value.
